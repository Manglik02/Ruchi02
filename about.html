About Us

PaintingWainting (PW) was conceptualized a couple of years back as a platform to help home artists. People pursuing painting as a hobby who neither knew the value of their creations nor someone someday who would buy their creations. Artwork is something like a tie, you can never give it away (since there are just so few takers nor dispose of them for the same reasons), and would be gathering dust in the remotest part of the house.

Our Team

Our Team is to give a platform to
artists pursuing Art as a hobby
and present their creations to the world.

Our Mission

Our Mission to help artists globally have a voice
for their work. To love and be loved for their accomplishments
